Biography

I received the B.S. degrees in mathematics and applied mathematics and the M.Eng. and Ph.D. degree in computer science from[Anhui University] of China in 2009, 2012, and 2015, respectively. I am currently a professor in [School of Computer Science and Technology] at [Anhui University].

My current research interests include image matching, graph data representation and learning and few-shot learning. I have published more than 100 papers including 60 papers in top conference and journal, such as IEEE T-PAMI, IJCV, IEEE T-MM, CVPR, NeurIPS and ACM-MM. I gained some research projects of NSFC and Natural Science Foundation of Anhui Province. I also received the rising star award of ACM Hefei.

安徽大学教授、硕士/博士生导师，安徽大学计算机学院副院长，安徽省计算机学会理事，安徽省计算机学会青工委副主任。从事结构模式识别和计算机视觉、医学影像分析以及AI4Science等方向的研究。 入选安徽省杰青人才项目，获安徽省计算机学会青年科学家奖。以第一/通讯作者在中科院一区、IEEE会刊和CCF-A类国际期刊会议上发表论文60篇（含第一/通讯作者发表人工智能顶级期刊T-PAMI, IJCV论文8篇）。 单篇论文谷歌学术最高引用420次。主持国家自然科学基金面上项目、安徽省重点研发计划项目、安徽省教育厅协同创新项目以及横向开发课题等。获安徽省计算机学会自然科学一等奖、ACM合肥分会新星奖等。
